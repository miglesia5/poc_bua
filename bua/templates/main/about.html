1. Purpose

This Section of the application is intended to provided an overview for a Bottoms Up Web app on the concept of Workforce management framework focus in a functional model, operational and culture based. From a Discovery phase to a mature management system state, touching components for leading and collaborating with different part and business units of share services in an enterprise environment. The section provides an understanding of workforce management and the pre work, culture

1.1 Description

Enterprise performances and services among business units depend not only in process and tools but on our workforce who leverage our knowledge capital, having a visibility support and proper management in our enterprise services creates a competitive advantage for our services to balance, forecast and harmonized staffing levels improving our services for customer and employee experiences. Workforce management includes more than a simple understanding of up coming workloads the web app offers a platform to deeply understand tasks, knowledge, process understanding, efforts, communications and more importantly people. We also consider that each business unit/services/squad has a different level of maturity level on business intelligent and workforces analytics as the enterprise evolves workforce management hopes to provide a framework and approach to focus workforce management at any business unit maturity stage.

1.2 Goal

The goal for workforce management is to establish a common culture, a management system set by key performance indicators harmonizing to the business day to day goals and objectives using best practices from Agile, Scrum, Project Management and continual improvement. The multiprogram should elaborate on Discovery Phase to vision the scope for any given business unit project. And followed by an iteration cero to initiate formalized and set up a diverse multifunctional team that will iterate in order to create a logic minimal viable product and elaborate to business goal for the business unit workforce management formal project The enterprise will be split in each business unit and among services creating a dashboard and support From a peer to peer bases generating synergy, bench marking and collaboration among areas and business units, creating a community and culture for workforce management among the business areas

2. Program

The value of the program rest in the services, mentorship, networking and recognition the business areas can gain by enrolling into workforce management. The focus is based into the capacity open and the value the business unit can generate as return for the investment. Aside of looking for logical space for a cross functional to elaborate on a program deliverable we will focus our efforts to create a co working culture on every opportunity not only in a local business unit level but also globally using the best at hand methodologies and technologies. The process for which the program will be encourage will be based on the following phases or concepts:

1. Awareness

2. Engagement

3. Program Development

4. Demo Program Closure

5. Retrospective and Re Engagement
